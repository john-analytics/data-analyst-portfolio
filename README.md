**Project 1: EPC Project Cost Control & Budget Variance Analysis**

**Objective:**
- To compare project budget vs actual cost and identify cost overruns in EPC projects.

**Tools Used:**

- Excel (Data cleaning, Pivot Tables)
- Power BI (Dashboards, KPIs)

**What I Did:**

- Cleaned project cost data in Excel
- Created pivot tables for project-wise and monthly summaries
- Calculated cost variance and variance %
- Built Power BI dashboards for management review

**Key Insights:**

- Identified over-budget projects early.

**Business Value:**
- Helped management track cost overruns and take early corrective actions.

#
**Project 2: EPC Project Progress Tracking & Delay Risk Analysis**

**Objective:**
- To track planned vs actual progress and identify delayed projects.

**Tools Used:**

- Excel (Data validation, Pivot Tables)

- Power BI (Progress & Risk Dashboards)

**What I Did:**

- Prepared progress data in Excel

- Calculated progress gap and delay days

- Classified projects into Low, Medium, and High risk

- Built dashboards to track execution status

**Key Insights:**

- Identified delayed projects

- Highlighted high-risk projects based on delay days

**Business Value:**
- Supported early decision-making to reduce project delays.
